About betta fish:

Betta fish, also known as Siamese fighting fish, are colorful and vibrant freshwater fish popular in home aquariums. They are known for their flowing fins and unique personalities. Betta fish are easy to care for and thrive best in warm, clean water with plenty of space to swim.

Betta fish tail types

  • Veiltail – Long, flowing downward tail
  • Crowntail – Spiky tail with extended rays
  • Halfmoon – Tail spreads 180° like a half-circle
  • Plakat – Short tail, resembles wild bettas
  • Double Tail – Two distinct tail lobes

Betta fish color types

  1. Solid Colors – Red, blue, white, black, yellow, etc
  2. Multicolor – A mix of 3+ colors
  3. Koi/Marble – Splotchy or changing colors
  4. Dragon Scale – Thick, metallic-looking scales
